About this episode

In Episode 6 of  A Little Throuple Tea , three gay men in a real-life throuple share the story of the one and only time they met another throuple in the wild — and why it completely changed how they see relationship dynamics.

From subtle red flags to a power dynamic that felt… off, this episode unpacks the moment we realized that not every throuple is healthy just because it’s three.

We talk about the feeling in your gut when something isn’t safe — even if you can’t explain why yet.

If you like queer podcasts that mix real relationship transparency, cultural commentary, and controlled chaos — this one hits different.
