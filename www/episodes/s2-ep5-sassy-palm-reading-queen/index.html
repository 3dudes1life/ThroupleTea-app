About this episode

Can your hands expose your entire relationship dynamic?

In Episode 5 of  A Little Throuple Tea , three gay men in a real-life throuple let the Sassy Palm Reading Queen take over and read what’s written in their actual palms.

And somehow… why does it all feel accurate?

It’s camp. It’s chaotic. It’s mildly spiritual.

And it was recorded on Valentine’s Day — so the energy was festive.

We break down what each palm says individually — and then what those lines reveal about how the three of us actually function together.
