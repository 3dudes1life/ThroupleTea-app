About this episode

In this episode of  A Little Throuple Tea , three gay men in a real-life throuple lean into curiosity, conspiracy-adjacent questions, and the kind of topics people love to side-eye but secretly think about anyway.

We’re talking aliens, other dimensions, parallel timelines, déjà vu, and things we can’t quite explain — not as experts or truth-tellers, but as three very curious people asking, “Okay but… what if?”

Just humor, curiosity, and a lot of unanswered questions.

• Why aliens feel less wild than half the things humans do

• Why government vagueness gives suspicious energy

• Whether extraterrestrials would even bother with Earth
