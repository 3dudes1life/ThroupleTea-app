About this episode

It’s the Season 1 Finale, and we’re going out the only way we know how — loud, messy, and reminiscing about the golden age of gay bars. 🌈

In Episode 10, we’re diving into gay bar culture from 2006–2009, the chaos before smartphones changed nightlife, and Will’s 15 years behind the DJ booth watching it all go down. From dance floor disasters to who actually survived a Sunday Funday… we lived through it and somehow came out functional. 😂

As a committed gay throuple, we’re also wrapping up Season 1 — the highs, the chaos, the inside jokes, and everything we didn’t plan but absolutely talked about anyway. If you’re into LGBTQ relationship podcasts, real-life throuple dynamics, and honest conversations about modern gay culture, this one’s for you.

Plus our final round of Smut TV and Social Tea to close out the season.
