About this episode

We finally escaped for a much-needed vacation to Lake Tahoe and Reno… and honestly, it was exactly what we needed.

This week we’re breaking down our entire Tahoe trip, from beautiful hikes and lake days to family time, casino adventures, Lime scooter rides, giant pine trees, and the reason we ended up extending the vacation instead of coming home.

We talk about what surprised us most about Tahoe, why Reno felt different this time, our favorite meals, favorite memories, and why this may have been one of the most relaxing trips we’ve taken in years.

We also answer a new Throuple Hotline question from a listener in Montreal:

📞 Have any of you ever second-guessed becoming a throuple?

📞 And does Daniel really wear socks during sex?
