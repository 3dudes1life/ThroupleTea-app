About this episode

Mercury’s in retrograde, emotions are in chaos, and apparently… don’t trust a unicorn. 💅

In this episode of our gay throuple podcast, we’re diving deep into birth charts, astrology, and how the stars might actually explain our personalities — and our LGBTQ relationship drama.

Daniel breaks down rising signs, sun and moon placements, and why he’s convinced the universe predicted this throuple. From love languages to reality TV smut, this one is equal parts cosmic and chaotic.

We debate which planet keeps messing with our Wi-Fi (spoiler: it’s always Mercury), whether astrology can predict compatibility in a polyamorous relationship, and why Will almost peed from laughing during this episode. 😂

If you’ve ever wondered how astrology affects relationships — especially in a three-person partnership — this one’s for you.
