About this episode

In Episode 9 of  A Little Throuple Tea , we’re breaking down how we actually manage money as a gay throuple — from budgeting three incomes into one household to figuring out who saves, who spends, and who absolutely cannot be trusted at Costco. 💸

We talk real-life TINK (Two Income, No Kids… plus one more 😏) finances, impulse spending, shared accounts, and how money dynamics affect relationships in a committed polyamorous triad.

🎲 A rapid-fire game that exposes way too much

Somehow this episode is financially responsible  and  completely unhinged.
