About this episode

Episode 2 of  A Little Throuple Tea  is here! This week, we’re answering your questions about our real-life gay throuple relationship — how we make a 3-person relationship work, boundaries, jealousy, dating, and what polyamory actually looks like behind the scenes.

We also catch up on life updates and dive into our weekly  Smut TV  segment (yes… we went there).

Heads up — this one’s a long ride (just over an hour), so buckle up. It’s messy, it’s honest, and it’s basically like hanging out on the couch with your favorite chaotic gay throuple.

If you’re curious about polyamory, throuple dynamics, LGBTQ relationships, or how three committed partners navigate real life together — this episode is for you.
