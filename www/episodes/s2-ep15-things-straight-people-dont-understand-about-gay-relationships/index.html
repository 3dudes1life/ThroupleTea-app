About this episode

What do straight people always ask gay couples and throuples?

This week on A Little Throuple Tea, we break down the funniest, weirdest, most common questions straight people ask about gay relationships.

Who’s the girl? Who’s the top? How do holidays work? Do your families know? How does a throuple even work?

We talk chosen family, communication, boundaries, stereotypes, why LGBTQ+ spaces matter, and the things people still don’t understand about queer relationships in 2026.

It gets chaotic, honest, funny, and a little unhinged… as usual.
