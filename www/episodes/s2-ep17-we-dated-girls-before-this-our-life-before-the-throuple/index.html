About this episode

Before the throuple, before California, before the chaos… we were just three very different kids growing up in very different worlds.

This week, we’re going all the way back — childhood memories, teenage chaos, first jobs, first relationships (yes… even the girl era), and the moments that shaped who we are today.

From desert lizard hunting to Midwest lake life… from religious upbringings to party years that probably should’ve gotten us arrested (okay, one of us actually did)… this episode is a full throwback.

We also get real about: • Coming out (and not always on your own terms) • Losing friends and finding yourself • The messy path that led us here • And what our younger selves would  actually  think about our lives now

PLUS… the Throuple Hotline is back with: • 🚨 Relationship red flags (or are they?) • 🍽️ The dish debate that almost started a war • 💀 A petty Costco revenge story that… honestly… we respect

And of course: 🔮 Sassy Astrology is SERVING this week 😑 Daniel’s dad joke… exists
