About this episode

This week we're diving into The Gay Rulebook We Never Received — the completely unofficial handbook that apparently every gay person got except us.

After a listener asked if they've broken a gay rule by never watching RuPaul's Drag Race, we started wondering: is there actually a list of rules we're all supposed to know? From gym culture and drag shows to Palm Springs, circuit parties, fashion, hookups, and stereotypes, we're unpacking the gay expectations we never signed up for.

We also talk about selling the Out at the Fair trailer, saying goodbye to a huge chapter of our lives, whether Caleb should grow a skullet, and why body hair deserves a little more respect.

Plus, we answer multiple Throuple Hotline questions from listeners navigating relationships, age gaps, friendship dynamics, and life as a throuple.

📞 Is it a gay crime to never watch RuPaul's Drag Race? 📞 How do you handle judgment as a throuple with a large age gap? 📞 What happens when a potential third starts pulling away?
