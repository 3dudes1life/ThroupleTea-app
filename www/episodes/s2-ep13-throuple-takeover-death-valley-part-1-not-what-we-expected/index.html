What is Death Valley actually like?

In Episode 13 of  A Little Throuple Tea , we take the podcast on the road — recording from our hotel room in Stovepipe Wells Village after the first few days of our trip.

This is Part 1 of our Death Valley series, where we break down everything we’ve experienced so far, what surprised us, and what the desert is actually like beyond the photos.

Over the first three days, we explored:

We talk about the drive out, the scale of the landscape, what felt surreal, what felt underwhelming, and the moments that made the trip worth it.
