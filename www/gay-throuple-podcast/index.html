What is A Little Throuple Tea?

A Little Throuple Tea is a gay relationship podcast hosted by a real-life gay throuple — three men in love talking openly about the real, funny, messy, and heartfelt parts of building a life together.

If you are searching for a gay throuple podcast, an LGBTQ relationship podcast, a throuple podcast, or a show about nontraditional queer love, welcome to the tea.

We are not relationship experts pretending to have a perfect formula. We are William, Caleb, and Daniel of 3Dudes1Life, and this is our actual relationship. We talk about communication, jealousy, money, family, travel, pop culture, chosen family, and the everyday chaos that happens when three men build a life together.

Why this podcast exists

Throuples are often treated like a punchline, a fantasy, or a scandal. We wanted to create something more honest than that. Our podcast shows the relationship part: the communication, the inside jokes, the arguments, the support, the planning, the public questions, and the deep love behind the chaos.

A gay throuple is not just a couple with a third person standing nearby. In our relationship, all three of us matter. All three of us have a voice. All three of us are part of the story. A Little Throuple Tea gives people a real-life look at what that can sound like.
